Survey-Oriented Research Map

This hub organizes UAV VLN literature into three main lines: traditional UAV navigation, Vision-Language-Action policy learning, and World Action Models that predict future observations or world states for planning. It also tracks navigation foundations, datasets, simulators, multimodal perception, evaluation metrics, and surveys.

Recommended Survey Structure

1. FoundationsVLN formulation, spatial grounding, navigation policy, instruction following.
2. Traditional UAV VLNSLAM, path planning, semantic maps, obstacle avoidance, visual navigation.
3. UAV VLAVision + language → action, action tokens, language-conditioned policy learning.
4. UAV WAMObservation + action → future observation/state for imagination-based planning.
5. Datasets & SimulatorsAirSim, Flightmare, Habitat, Isaac Sim, instruction-trajectory-video data.
6. EvaluationSR, SPL, collision rate, instruction accuracy, sim-to-real and OOD generalization.
